Our Journey

Impact Timeline

Key milestones that mark our journey of creating lasting impact in communities.

2015

Organization Founded

HopeRise Foundation was established with a mission to transform lives through quality education and community empowerment.

2017

10,000 Beneficiaries

Reached our first milestone of 10,000 direct beneficiaries across education and health programs in rural communities.

2019

Multi-State Expansion

Expanded operations to multiple states, establishing regional offices and partnering with local organizations.

2021

Digital Education Launch

Launched digital education initiatives, bringing technology-enabled learning to remote and underserved areas.

2023

500,000+ Lives Impacted

Crossed the milestone of impacting over half a million lives through our comprehensive community programs.

2025

International Partnerships

Expanded international partnerships, collaborating with global organizations to scale our proven impact models.
