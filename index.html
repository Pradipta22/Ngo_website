Tell the Story

Why India Still Needs Change

While progress has been made, millions still face critical barriers to basic survival and dignity. Here is where we make an impact.

🎓

Education Gap

Over 6 million children in India are still out of school. Rural education suffers from poor infrastructure and inadequate teaching support.

🩺

Healthcare Access

More than 70% of India's population lives in rural areas, yet they only have access to 30% of the country's healthcare facilities.

🌿

Climate Change

Unpredictable rain and rising heat stress affect rural agriculture, water security, and resource systems directly.

Our Programs

Your Volunteer Journey

Follow our seamless, structured path to becoming an integral part of our community family.

📝

1. Register

Submit your profile and share your area of skills (teaching, healthcare, digital support).

🎓

2. Training

Undergo a thorough onboarding and skill matching session led by seasoned coordinators.

🏘️

3. Visit Community

Spend time in rural villages directly connecting with children and local self-help groups.

🤝

4. Volunteer

Actively lead teaching camps, health drives, tree plantations, or remote skill campaigns.

❤️

5. Become Family

Build lifelong bonds with our field workers and communities, becoming part of the HopeRise network.

Our Journey

Impact Timeline

Key milestones that mark our journey of creating lasting impact in communities.

2015

Organization Founded

HopeRise Foundation was established with a mission to transform lives through quality education and community empowerment.

2017

10,000 Beneficiaries

Reached our first milestone of 10,000 direct beneficiaries across education and health programs in rural communities.

2019

Multi-State Expansion

Expanded operations to multiple states, establishing regional offices and partnering with local organizations.

2021

Digital Education Launch

Launched digital education initiatives, bringing technology-enabled learning to remote and underserved areas.

2023

500,000+ Lives Impacted

2025

International Partnerships

Expanded international partnerships, collaborating with global organizations to scale our proven impact models.
